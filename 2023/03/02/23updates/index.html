I hate reality

I haven’t made any progress in so called mindset compared to last year. I simply cope by taking medication, saying “Sorry my health condition is bad”, and avoiding reality altogether. I know the saying, “You don’t need to be fully prepared to do anything,” but I don’t even want to do anything anymore. I’m just too disappointed.

One of the things that disappoints me is my lack of progress in my coding skills and my artwork. I feel like I’m not good enough, and no matter how much time and effort I put into it, I just can’t seem to get any better. It’s frustrating.

But it’s not just my lack of progress that’s getting to me. It’s the constant pressure to be social, to be emotionally available, and to always be “on.” I feel like I’m constantly bombarded with stimuli, and it’s overwhelming.

And even when things are going well in my life, I still feel exhausted. I’m too fragile, too easily broken.
